About 7ArtZone

Built to Create.
Born to Entertain.

7ArtZone Entertainment Pvt. Ltd. is a production and technology company headquartered in Ahmedabad, Gujarat. We take our name from the seven classical arts — film, music, dance, painting, sculpture, architecture and literature.

From cinematic VFX and CGI to original music, feature films, web series and large-scale live events, we operate across the full creative spectrum with a team of over 100 specialist crew.

We have invested in high-performance rendering infrastructure and a dedicated Ahmedabad headquarters — building toward becoming one of India's most capable independent production studios.
